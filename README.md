# 🕯️ Beacon — A Survivor-Centered Safety & Support Platform

## 🌍 Overview

Beacon is a survivor-centered digital platform designed to provide safe, accessible, and privacy-focused support for individuals experiencing vulnerable situations.

Built for **Call for Code**, Beacon focuses on using technology responsibly to reduce barriers to support, improve access to resources, and empower survivors with information and choices while protecting their privacy.

The platform is designed around the principle that survivors should remain in control of their own journey — with safety, consent, and dignity at the core.

---

## 💡 Problem Statement

Many survivors face difficulties accessing support due to:
- Lack of awareness about available resources
- Fear of judgment or exposure
- Privacy concerns
- Difficulty navigating support systems

Beacon aims to bridge this gap by providing a secure and supportive digital experience.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🛡️ Survivor-Centered Design
- Prioritizes survivor safety, privacy, and autonomy
- Provides support without judgment or pressure
- Keeps users in control of their decisions

### 🔐 Privacy-First Approach
- Minimizes unnecessary data collection
- Protects sensitive user information
- Encourages informed consent before sharing information

### 🤖 AI-Assisted Support
- Uses AI capabilities to provide helpful guidance
- Helps users navigate available resources
- Designed with responsible AI principles

### 🌎 Accessible Support
- Provides clear information and guidance
- Reduces barriers to finding help
- Designed for a simple and approachable user experience

---

## 🧠 Responsible AI & Ethics

Beacon follows responsible AI principles:

### Consent
Users decide what information they provide and how it is used.

### Privacy
The system avoids collecting unnecessary personal information and prioritizes data protection.

### Transparency
Users should understand how the platform works and how their information is handled.

### Human-Centered Design
Technology supports survivors but does not replace human support systems.

---

## ⚖️ Privacy & Consent Trade-offs

A key design challenge was balancing personalization with privacy.

Collecting additional user information could enable more customized assistance, but it may increase risks associated with sensitive data exposure. Beacon prioritizes survivor safety by limiting data collection and ensuring users maintain control over their information.
